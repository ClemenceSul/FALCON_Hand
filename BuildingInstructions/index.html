FALCON Hand Building Instructions

These building instructions have been designed to help you build the FALCON Hand. The total build should take you about 48 hours, but this can vary depending on your level of experience.

This Fully Actuated, Life-like, Compact OpeN-source (FALCON) hand has been designed for research purposes. It has been developed to make it’s complex system simple to assemble, leveraging 3D printing and readily-available components, for a total cost of £240. The FALCON Hand is versatile with 20 degrees of freedom, and similar grasping capabilities as a human hand. With the implementation of a pulley differential mechanism in the fingers, it can grasp objects with an enveloping motion, maximising contact surface area for a stronger grip. The embedded joint sensors provide valuable feedback regarding the finger’s position and optimises control. The FALCON Hand’s design has been validated with thorough testing, and presents greater general characteristics, comparable grasping abilities to a human and a building difficulty adapted for beginners.
